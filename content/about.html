From networking to debugging, my work ethic has served me well in my commitment to follow my curiosty and pursue my passion for learning. In addition to giving me access to a great curriculum, ADA has taught me how to feel comfortable with the uncomfortable, and that has been the key to my success and a great daily reminder. Hence, now I am confident in pursuing my interests in full-stack based work. I look forward to bringing my technical and transferrable skills into the workforce and take ownership of my accomplishments and contribute to team goals.
